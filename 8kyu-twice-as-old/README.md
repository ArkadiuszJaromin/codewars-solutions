## 8 kyu - Twice as Old
https://www.codewars.com/kata/5b853229cfde412a470000d0/csharp

Calculates how many years ago (or in how many years) the father was/will be
twice as old as his son. The age difference between father and son is
constant over time, so the answer simplifies to:

`Math.Abs(dadYears - 2 * sonYears)`
